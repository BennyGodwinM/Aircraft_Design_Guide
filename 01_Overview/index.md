# Chapter 1 — Overview of Aircraft Design

*Philosophy, Process, and the Challenges of Bringing Aircraft to Life*

---

This chapter introduces aircraft design as a discipline — what it is, how it is approached, and why it is hard. It is deliberately non-technical: no equations, no aerodynamics. The goal is to build the right mental model before any of the technical content begins.

## Sections

1. **[What is Aircraft Design?](01_what_is_design)** — Three foundational definitions and what they share
2. **[The Philosophy of Design](02_philosophy)** — Five principles that guide every design decision, and the iterative loop
3. **[The Three-Phase Process](03_design_phases)** — Conceptual, preliminary, and detailed design, and why the sequence matters
4. **[The Interdisciplinary Nature](04_interdisciplinary)** — Why no single discipline can be resolved in isolation
5. **[The Hurdles of Aircraft Design](05_hurdles)** — Structural challenges every designer must navigate, and the mission profile that ties them together
6. **[Summary](06_summary)** — Key principles in one table, and what comes next
