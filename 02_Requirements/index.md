# Section 2 — Stakeholders, Requirements & Systems Engineering

**PART I — FOUNDATIONS & CONTEXT**

> *Who has a stake in this vehicle, what do they need, and how do those needs become engineering requirements?*

No aircraft gets built without someone deciding — in precise, measurable terms —
what it must do, for whom, and under what conditions. This section covers the full
chain from stakeholder identification through mission definition, requirements
engineering, the systems engineering process, and the design review cycle that
governs programme progress.
